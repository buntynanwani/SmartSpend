#### **1\. Core Financials (The Big Picture)**

| Measure Name | What it does (Plain English) |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Total Spend** | The foundational measure. The absolute total amount of money spent across any selected timeframe, category, or shop. |
| **YTD Spend** | "Year-to-Date" spending. Calculates the cumulative total spent from January 1st of the current year up to the current date. |
| **Monthly Spend** | The total money spent strictly within the current calendar month you are looking at. |

#### **2\. Basket & Volume Analytics (Shopping Behavior)**

| Measure Name | What it does (Plain English) |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Number of Purchases** | The total count of distinct shopping trips or individual receipts. |
| **Total Units/Items** | The physical volume or quantity of items bought (e.g., number of bananas, number of protein bars). |
| **Avg Basket Size** | The average amount of money spent per individual trip to the supermarket/store. |
| **Avg Weekly Grocery** | The average amount of money spent specifically on food/groceries per week. |
| **User Share %** | The percentage of the total household/system spending that belongs to one specific user profile. |

#### **3\. Advanced Pricing Intelligence (The Money Savers)**

| Measure Name | What it does (Plain English) |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Weighted Avg Unit Price** | The *true* average cost of an item. It divides the total money spent by the total quantity bought, giving a 100% accurate price point regardless of varying receipt totals. |
| **Price per KG** | Scales the unit price up to a full kilogram to make it readable for human analysis (fixing the issue of microscopic prices for items bought in grams). |
| **Cheapest Shop for Product** | A dynamic text engine that evaluates all shops and returns the name of the merchant offering the lowest historical price for a selected item. |
| **Price Difference %** | Calculates the inflation or gap between the most expensive shop and the cheapest shop for the same product. |

#### **4\. Time Intelligence (Trend Tracking)**

| Measure Name | What it does (Plain English) |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Prev Month Spend** | Looks back in time to calculate exactly what was spent in the previous calendar month. |
| **MoM Change %** | "Month-over-Month". The percentage increase (red) or decrease (green) in spending compared to the previous month. |
| **Rolling 3M Avg** | A smoothed-out average of spending over the last 90 days, which helps identify true financial trends while ignoring single expensive outlier months. |

#### **5\. Health & Wellness Tracking (Specialized Logic)**

| Measure Name | What it does (Plain English) |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Is Health Category** | A logical background flag (1 or 0\) that instantly filters the dashboard to show only wellness investments (therapy, vitamins, protein, etc.) without manual clicking. |
| **Health Spend YTD** | The total amount of money invested strictly into physical and mental well-being since January 1st. |
| **Psychologist Sessions** | A specific counter tracking the exact number of clinical therapy appointments attended. |
